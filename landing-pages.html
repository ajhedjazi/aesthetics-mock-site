Use cases

Where landing pages usually help most.

Single treatment campaigns

Ideal when one treatment needs a focused message instead of getting buried inside a full website.

Offer-led traffic

Useful for paid campaigns, launch offers, or consultation pushes where clarity matters more than variety.

Stronger booking flow

A single page can remove friction for visitors who already know what they are interested in.

Why they work

One message, one offer and one CTA usually convert more calmly.

Less distraction

Visitors do not need to work through a full service menu to understand what the page is about.

Stronger relevance

The whole page can be aligned to one treatment, one launch or one campaign source.

Cleaner momentum

Every section can support the same next step instead of splitting attention across too many options.
